# Privacy Policy for This APP

**Last updated: April 2026**

### 1. Data Collection
This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location). We believe your routine should remain private.

### 2. Local Storage
All data created within the app is stored exclusively on your local device. We do not use external servers to host your content. 

* **Note:** If you delete the app or clear its data, your local checklists will be permanently removed.

### 3. Third-Party Services
Payments and digital purchases are securely processed through the **Google Play Billing** system. LastCheck does not have access to your credit card information, bank details, or financial records.

### 4. Privacy by Design
This App is designed to work offline. What happens in your home stays on your phone. No cloud, no tracking, just peace of mind.

---
*For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us through the Play Store developer contact.*
